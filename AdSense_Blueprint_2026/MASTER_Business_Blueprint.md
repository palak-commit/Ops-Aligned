## PHASE 1 — FINAL NICHE SELECTION

**Selected Niche: Notion Ecosystem Implementation (Stage 1 of OpsAligned)**

After analyzing the feedback and refining the strategy for maximum long-term survival against Google updates, the approach shifts from "broad SaaS tech" to **ultra-narrow topical authority**. 

### Why This Niche Wins
* **Hyper-Focused Authority**: By launching with ONLY Notion (100-150 implementation articles), we signal to Google that we are the definitive subject matter expert on this single entity before expanding horizontally.
* **Expansion Roadmap**: 
  - Stage 1: Notion (100-150 articles)
  - Stage 2: Zapier (100 articles)

## PHASE 5 — SEO ARCHITECTURE

* **Entity SEO & Semantic Clusters**: Treating the site as a database of entities. Every article maps perfectly to a node in the Notion Content Graph.
* **Original Assets Strategy**: Google Image Search will be a primary acquisition channel. NEVER use stock images. Every post requires custom architecture diagrams, flow charts, and annotated UI before/afters.
* **Original Research (The Backlink Engine)**: Every 20-30 articles, publish data-driven content to naturally attract backlinks (e.g., "We Benchmarked 25 Notion CRM Automations," "Fastest Webhook Tools 2026").
* **Internal Linking Strategy**: Strict siloing within the software ecosystem before interlinking across different software (which only happens in Stage 2+).

## PHASE 7 — THE WORKFLOW LIBRARY TEMPLATE & SCORING SYSTEM

### The Asset Requirements
Every published workflow MUST include:
1. Written implementation guide.
2. 2-5 minute screen recording (YouTube embed).
3. Custom architecture diagram (e.g., Gmail -> AI Summary -> Notion).
4. Annotated screenshots.
5. Downloadable asset (Template or JSON snippet).
6. FAQ Schema.

### The 100-Point Content Scoring System
*No article is published unless it scores 90/100 or higher.*

* **Search intent matching**: 15 pts
* **EEAT (Expertise/Experience)**: 15 pts
* **Original screenshots & diagrams**: 10 pts
* **Internal links (Content Graph)**: 10 pts
* **External references (Authoritative)**: 5 pts
* **Helpful Content (No fluff)**: 15 pts
* **Readability (8th grade, scannable)**: 10 pts
* **Mobile UX**: 5 pts
* **Schema Markup**: 5 pts
* **Performance (CWV)**: 5 pts
* **CTA Placement (Newsletter/Template)**: 5 pts

### Strategic AI Usage
AI is used for: Keyword clustering, outline generation, FAQ extraction, schema creation, and regex generation.
AI is explicitly NOT used for: Writing the article text, taking screenshots, or forming recommendations. Experience must be human.

## PHASE 8 — MAINTENANCE, UPDATES, AND ADSENSE ROADMAP

* **AdSense Approval Strategy**: Follow the 90-day publishing schedule. Apply once the site has 40+ high-scoring articles, passing Core Web Vitals, and active organic traffic.
* **The Freshness Maintenance Schedule**: SaaS changes fast. Decaying content loses traffic.
  - *High-traffic tutorials*: Audited every 3 months.
  - *Medium-traffic tutorials*: Audited every 6 months.
  - *Low-traffic tutorials*: Audited annually.
* **Audit Checklist**: Check for UI changes, deprecated API endpoints, new feature rollouts, and pricing updates.
