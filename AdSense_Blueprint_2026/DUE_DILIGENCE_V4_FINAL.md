## STAGE 1 — MARKET VALIDATION

**Verified:**
* **Commercial intent**: High. B2B implementation workflows target businesses with budgets.
* **RPM potential**: High. B2B SaaS traffic commands premium AdSense rates ($20-$50+ RPMs).
