# Phase 8: Website Blueprint & Phase 9: AdSense Roadmap

## 1. Ideal Publishing Website Blueprint (2026 Standards)

### Core Pages Required
1. **Homepage**: Clear navigation, categorized latest posts, author trust signals.
2. **Category Pages**: Don't just list posts. Add a custom intro paragraph describing the category for topical authority.
3. **Article Pages**: Clean layout, no sidebar on mobile, sticky table of contents on desktop, author box at the bottom.
4. **Author Page**: Detailed biography, credentials, social links, and a list of all posts by that author.
5. **Trust Pages**:
   - About Us (Story, Mission, Team)
   - Contact (Email, Physical address, form)
   - Privacy Policy & Terms of Service
   - Editorial Policy & Fact Checking Policy (Crucial for YMYL topics)

### Technical Components
- **Navigation**: Keep it simple. Max 5-6 top-level items.
- **Footer**: Repeat main categories, link all Trust Pages, copyright info.
- **Search Functionality**: A visible search bar in the header.
- **Breadcrumbs**: Enabled on all posts and categories (Home > Category > Post).
- **XML & HTML Sitemaps**: Essential for indexing.
- **Ads.txt**: Required file placed in the root directory once AdSense is approved.
- **Branding**: Clean logo, consistent favicon, cohesive color palette (max 3 main colors).

### Performance & Accessibility
- Caching plugin configured (e.g., WP Rocket, LiteSpeed).
- Image lazy loading enabled.
- All links have distinct colors and hover states.
- High contrast text (black/dark grey on white).

## 2. The 90-Day AdSense Approval Roadmap

### Month 1: Foundation & Trust (Days 1-30)
- **Week 1**: Domain purchase, hosting setup (fast host like Cloudways or Kinsta), WordPress installation, lightweight theme (GeneratePress, Kadence, or Astra).
- **Week 2**: Create all Core Pages and Trust Pages (About, Contact, Privacy, Terms). Setup Google Analytics and Search Console.
- **Week 3**: Finalize site architecture and categories. Publish first 5 pillar articles (1,500+ words each).
- **Week 4**: Publish 5 more articles. Ensure all images are custom or properly credited.

### Month 2: Content Velocity (Days 31-60)
- **Week 5**: Publish 5 articles (Total 15). Focus on low-competition, informational keywords to trigger initial indexing.
- **Week 6**: Internal linking audit. Ensure every new post links to a previous post.
- **Week 7**: Publish 5 articles (Total 20). Setup social media profiles (Twitter, Pinterest) and share posts to generate initial social signals.
- **Week 8**: Publish 5 articles (Total 25). Run a technical audit (check for 404s, mobile responsiveness, page speed).

### Month 3: Preparation & Application (Days 61-90)
- **Week 9**: Publish 5 articles (Total 30). You now have enough content for an AdSense review.
- **Week 10**: The Pre-Application Audit.
   - Check Google Search Console: Are all 30 pages indexed?
   - Check traffic: Are you getting at least 10-20 organic visitors a day?
   - Verify there are absolutely no empty categories, broken links, or placeholder text.
- **Week 11**: Apply for Google AdSense. Add the AdSense code to your site header.
- **Week 12**: Continue publishing 2-3 articles per week while waiting. Do not stop publishing during the review process (it shows Google the site is active).

*Note: Approval can take anywhere from 3 days to 4 weeks. If rejected, do not immediately reapply. Read the rejection reason, fix the issue, publish 5 more high-quality articles, and wait 2 weeks before reapplying.*
