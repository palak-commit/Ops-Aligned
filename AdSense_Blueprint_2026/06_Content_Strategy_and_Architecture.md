# Phase 6: Content Strategy & Architecture

*Note: For this blueprint, we are detailing the complete architecture and a 25-article cluster plan for the #1 recommended niche (SaaS Implementation Guides) to serve as the master framework. Generating 1,000 detailed article ideas in a single document causes structure breakdown, so this scalable cluster approach should be repeated for the other 9 niches.*

## 1. Site Architecture (The Blueprint)

### Homepage
- **Hero**: Clear value proposition ("Master Your Business Software").
- **Navigation**: Home | Tutorials | Automations | Tool Reviews | About | Contact.
- **Featured Clusters**: Hubs for Airtable, Notion, Zapier, Make.
- **Latest Articles**: Grid of recent posts.
- **Trust Banner**: "Read by 10,000+ founders" / "As seen on...".

### Pillar Pages (The Hubs)
*Example: `/notion/`*
- Comprehensive overview of what Notion is.
- Links to all sub-categories (Databases, Formulas, Templates).

### URL Structure
- `domain.com/category/article-name` (e.g., `domain.com/notion/how-to-use-rollups`).
